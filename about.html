About

Hi, I'm Franchesca

Founder of Be You For You — a community initiative under Monet's, built for Jacksonville women who want a sisterhood, not just a night out.

My Story

Fashion designer. Marketing manager. Community founder.

I hold a B.S. in Fashion Merchandising & Retailing and an MBA in Operations & Supply Chain Management, and I've spent 5+ years in marketing and leadership roles. Be You For You lives at the intersection of all of it — a brand-minded, operations-backed community built with real care.

Be You For You is a transformative community designed to empower women of all ages, shapes, and sizes here in Jacksonville, Florida. It's a space to embark on a journey of self-discovery — one that embraces both outer beauty and inner strength, and builds a confidence that radiates from within rather than one measured against anyone else. Everything we do centers on three themes: Empower, Embrace, and Elevate — creating an environment where women can explore their unique beauty and grow into the most confident version of themselves.

At its heart, BYFY is a sisterhood: a safe space of belonging for women who are looking for their people — others who enjoy the same activities, think the same way, and want real connection outside of clubbing and nightlife-only socializing.

Our Journey

From One Brunch to a Movement

A year of monthly programming, built one event at a time.

March 2025

The Inaugural Meet & Greet Brunch

The very first BYFY gathering — the moment the idea became a real, in-person community.

May – October 2025

Finding Our Rhythm

Aerial yoga, a spa day with Skyyn Aesthetics, our 1st Annual Rooftop Yoga signature event, Mimosas & Mortgages, and Fun on the Fairway — our first golf mixer collab.

January – February 2026

Be You Be Still & For the Gals

A stretch-and-social reset to open the year, followed by a candle-making evening built for connection over craft.

March 2026

Her Space: 1st Anniversary Expo

A full experiential expo at Serenity Restaurant & Lounge — an Activation Passport, networking bingo, a sip & sparkle bar, and a room full of local vendors celebrating one year of BYFY.

Now

Building What's Next

A Founders Council to share ownership of BYFY's future, a growing calendar of collabs, and a long-term vision to grow our anniversary event into an annual conference.
