The Recap

Year One & Two, In Order

Every event that's brought this community closer together.

March 23, 2025

Coming of Spring Women's Brunch

A garden tea party brunch celebrating Women's Month — a curated flower bar, empowerment conversations, and affirmation cards to carry into spring.

May 31, 2025

Aerial Yoga Party

Our first movement-based event, done a little differently — upside down and all.

June 22, 2025

Meet & Greet Brunch

More faces, more friendships, more momentum for the growing BYFY community.

July 19, 2025

1st Annual Rooftop Yoga

A rooftop flow at SIP Rooftop Lounge followed by a wellness social hour with vendor activations and mimosas — the event that put BYFY on the map.

August 30, 2025

Mimosas & Mortgages: First Time Home Buyers Brunch

Wellness meets financial literacy — brunch with a purpose for women navigating their first home purchase.

September 28, 2025

The SKYYN Ritual: Luxury Spa Day

A collab built around rest, recovery, and self-care.

October 27, 2025

SIP & Screen

A cozy movie-night mixer — proof BYFY shows up for every kind of gathering.

January 24, 2026

Be You Be Still: Stretch Class

A guided stretch and mindfulness reset at Jacksonville Centre of the Arts to open the new year with calm and clarity.

February 23, 2026

For The Gals Candle Making Class

A hands-on candle-making evening in Jacksonville Beach with Wick & Twisted — craft as connection.

March 28, 2026

Anniversary Expo

Her Space: Be You For You Women's Expo at Serenity Restaurant & Lounge — a curated collection of interactive brand experiences celebrating our anniversary and Jacksonville's women-owned businesses.

May 16, 2026

2nd Annual Rooftop Yoga

Back on a rooftop at Union Terminal Warehouse for round two — yoga, wellness vendors, and VIP swag bags with panoramic city views.
