Why It Exists

Real Ownership, Not Just an Invite List

As BYFY has grown, one thing became clear: the women who show up most consistently should have a real hand in shaping what comes next. The Founders Council gives our most engaged members ownership over event ideation and execution — while Franchesca retains final approval on budget and brand, keeping BYFY's voice consistent as it scales.

It's governance built for a community, not a corporation — clear enough to avoid confusion, flexible enough to grow with us.

How It Works

Council vs. Founder: Who Owns What

The clearest part of the Founders Council Charter — and the most important.

Council Owns

Ideation & Execution

Event concepts, themes, vendor outreach, day-of logistics, and the creative energy behind what BYFY does next.

Founder Retains

Budget & Brand Approval

Final sign-off on spend and brand consistency — so BYFY stays true to its mission as more voices join the table.

What You Get

Flexibility

A Natural Off-Ramp
