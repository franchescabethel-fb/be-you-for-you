About Be You For You

A Community for Women Who Want More

Be You, For You is a women's empowerment and lifestyle community that hosts curated events, workshops, and experiences designed to help women embrace their personal power, connect with like-minded individuals, and elevate their lives. Whether through fashion, wellness, or meaningful conversations, we are dedicated to celebrating every woman's journey toward confidence and success.

The Community's 3 Objectives
01 · Objective

Confidence & Self-Expression

Spaces designed to help you show up exactly as you are — no pretense, no competition, just room to be fully yourself.

02 · Objective

Wellness & Personal Growth

From rooftop yoga to candle-making to meaningful conversation — experiences built around your growth, not just your calendar.

03 · Objective

Community & Connection

A network of women who show up for each other — and for the local Jacksonville businesses and creatives who show up for us.

Hi, I'm Franchesca

Founder, Be You For You

I'm the founder of Be You For You, a community initiative under Monet's built to give Jacksonville women a real sisterhood — one that doesn't require a night out to feel it. Between a background in fashion merchandising, an MBA in operations, and years leading marketing teams, I built BYFY to be the place I always wished existed.

What started as a Meet & Greet Brunch in March 2025 has grown into a full year of monthly programming and our first Anniversary Expo. There's a lot more coming — I'd love for you to be part of it.
