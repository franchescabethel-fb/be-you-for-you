If you're craving a community that feels like home…

You've found your people.

Be You For You (BYFY) brings women of all ages together in a safe sisterhood — to be themselves, connect with like-minded women outside of clubbing and nightlife, and experience curated events that expose them to the best of local Jacksonville. No pretense. No competition. Just real connection.

01 · Connection

Real Friendships

Small enough to actually meet people, big enough to always have somewhere to belong. Every event is designed for genuine conversation, not just a crowd.

02 · Curated Experiences

Monthly Events

From rooftop yoga to candle-making to golf mixers — every month brings a new, intentional way to show up for yourself and your girls.

03 · Local Discovery

Community Partners

BYFY events spotlight Jacksonville-area wellness studios, boutiques, and small businesses — so members discover their city while they discover each other.

Hi, I'm Franchesca

Founder, Be You For You

I'm the founder of Be You For You, a community initiative under Monet's built to give Jacksonville women a real sisterhood — one that doesn't require a night out to feel it. Between a background in fashion merchandising, an MBA in operations, and years leading marketing teams, I built BYFY to be the place I always wished existed.

What started as a Meet & Greet Brunch in March 2025 has grown into a full year of monthly programming and our first Anniversary Expo. There's a lot more coming — I'd love for you to be part of it.
